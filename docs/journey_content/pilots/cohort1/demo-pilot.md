## Overview

Cross-border pilot connecting existing urban digital twins for air quality and mobility monitoring, enabling interoperable data exchange between two European cities.

## Use Cases

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-weather-windy:{ .lg .middle } **Air Quality Monitoring**

    ---

    Real-time air quality data exchange between Lisbon and Luxembourg urban digital twins.

-   :material-bus-clock:{ .lg .middle } **Urban Mobility**

    ---

    Cross-border mobility data sharing to improve traffic management and public transport integration.

</div>
